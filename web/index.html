Methodology
  • Block tariffs: kWh up to (threshold × days) billed at the low rate, remainder at the high rate, matching your current GLOSAVE structure.
  • Single rate tariffs: all usage billed at one rate (high rate ignored).
  • Time of use tariffs: usage split by the "peak share" slider between the low (off-peak) and high (peak) rates — an approximation, since real TOU billing depends on the actual time energy is used.
  • Discount % is an ongoing conditional discount (e.g. pay-on-time, direct debit) applied to the subtotal — separate from one-off sign-up credits, which are noted per-plan but not counted as recurring savings.
